About

bitCHF currency

ERC20 main token which replicates the value of the Swiss Franc currency.

bitCHF share

ERC20 token for investors which periodically distributes dividends

bitCHF convertible bond

ERC20 token designed to help controlling the price of the cryptocurrency



bitCHF is a prototype for easy accessible, exchangeable and tradable stablecoin which target is to replicate the value of Swiss Franc currency.

The stability is provided with crypto-collateralization and trading methods triggered algorithmically.

The system is based on three types of financial instruments. The main token bitCHF is the crypto-currency. Share and convertible bond are designed to provide investors with revenues and to control the price of the bitCHF currency on the other hand.
